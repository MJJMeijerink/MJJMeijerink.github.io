Research paper with My Japanese Coach

Researching an interactive game.

For Research of interactive systems we were supposed to research either the consequences of serious games, visual analytics or robots. We chose to research a Serious game. Serious games are games made to entertain and teach people. We decided to use My Japanese Coach as our serious game. It's a game that teaches you Japanese. We studied the effects of this game compared to traditional language learning.
