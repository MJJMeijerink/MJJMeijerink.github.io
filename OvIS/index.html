Research paper with My Japanese Coach

For Onderzoek van Interactieve Systemen (Research of interactive systems), we were supposed to research either the effects of serious games, visual analytics or robots. We chose to research a Serious game. Serious games are games made to entertain and teach people. We decided to use My Japanese Coach as our serious game. It's a game that teaches you Japanese. We studied the effects of this game compared to traditional language learning.
